Idaho, USA

Idaho is a state in the Northwest of the United States, and is bordered by Montana, Wyoming, Utah, Nevada, Oregon, Washington, and Canada. It's home to beautiful mountain ranges, hills, and canyons with diverse and abundant wildlife. It's capital is Boise, and the state heads development on new technologies via the Idaho National Labratory, or INL. Many know the state by it's production of potatoes for the whole nation.
