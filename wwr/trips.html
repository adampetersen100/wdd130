Explore before the Experience!

In the beautiful plains of Idaho, we will explore the captivating and lively Snake River! For 3 days, you will be taken on a majestic excursion with one of our expert river guides, enjoy the quick rapids and slow flows and witness firsthand why Idaho is called the gem state!

Within it's towering mountains and vast deserts, Utah is also known for its large bodies of water. This includes the Colorado river, which spans multiple states. Witness the imposing landscape of the canyon walls surrounding the Colorado river!

In the winding roads of the Colorado mountains, the Arkansas river offers both leisurely flows, and heart-pounding sections for every rafter out there! Get lost in the labrynth of mountains and valleys in This gorgeous display of nature and her beauty!
