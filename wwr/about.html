History

Founded by a BYU-I student with a passion for riding the rapids, Rowdy Rapids Rafting fresh company was born in 2023. From humble beginnings, it has grown to be a trusted source of safe and fun rafting! The culture of the company has grown, but will always maintain the standard of wholesome, adventure-filled fun! With this standard in mind, we have grown and will continue to become East Idaho's #1 source of family-friendly fun in the outdoors!
